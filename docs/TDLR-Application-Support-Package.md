## Executive Summary

Texas DriveEd is developing a modern, fully compliant online driver education platform for Texas. Our curriculum is explicitly aligned with the **Program of Organized Instruction in Driver Education and Traffic Safety (POI-DE)** and incorporates all **15 mandatory statutory topics** required by Texas Education Code Chapter 1001 §§102–1101.

The platform features robust time-tracking, audit logging, certificate issuance with control numbers, and electronic reporting readiness for TDLR’s Online Licensing System.

This package demonstrates our readiness to become a TDLR-approved Driver Education provider.

## 3. Statutory Required Topics Compliance

All 15 topics from Texas Education Code Chapter 1001 §§102–1101 are covered with dedicated instructional time and assessments. See the detailed mapping in `docs/statutory-required-topics.md`.

**Summary of Coverage:**
- Heavy emphasis in Modules 1, 6, 7, 8, 10, and 12
- Treated as non-optional completion gates in the platform
- Separate audit logging and reporting capability
