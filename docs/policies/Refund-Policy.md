# Refund and Cancellation Policy

**Texas DriveEd**  
**Effective Date:** May 25, 2026

## 1. Purpose

This policy outlines the terms under which students may cancel enrollment or request refunds for Texas DriveEd courses.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all students enrolling in Texas DriveEd online driver education courses (Teen and Adult programs).

## 3. Cancellation by Student

Students may cancel their enrollment at any time by contacting support@texasdrived.com or through their student dashboard.

### Refund Eligibility

| Time Since Enrollment | Refund Amount | Conditions |
|-----------------------|---------------|------------|
| Within 3 days         | 100%          | No lessons started |
| 4–14 days             | 75%           | Less than 25% of course completed |
| 15–30 days            | 50%           | Less than 50% of course completed |
| After 30 days or >50% complete | 0%     | No refund |

**Exceptions:**
- If a student has already received their DE-964 certificate, no refund will be issued.
- Refunds will not be issued for courses purchased through third-party partners unless otherwise specified at time of purchase.

## 4. Cancellation by Texas DriveEd

Texas DriveEd reserves the right to cancel a student’s enrollment for:
- Violation of the Student Code of Conduct
- Fraudulent activity or misrepresentation
- Technical abuse of the platform

In such cases, a prorated refund may be issued at the sole discretion of Texas DriveEd.

## 5. Refund Processing

- Approved refunds will be processed within 10–14 business days.
- Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.
- A confirmation email will be sent once the refund has been processed.

## 6. Course Transfers

Students may request to transfer their enrollment to a future course start date (if applicable) instead of receiving a refund. Such requests are subject to availability and approval.

## 7. Contact

For refund or cancellation requests, please contact:  
**Email:** support@texasdrived.com  
**Subject Line:** Refund Request – [Student Name]

## 8. Policy Changes

Texas DriveEd reserves the right to update this policy. Changes will not affect enrollments made prior to the effective date of the change.
