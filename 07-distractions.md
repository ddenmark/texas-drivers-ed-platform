# Module 6: Environmental Factors
**Target Duration:** 3.0 hours (180 minutes)  
**Position:** Module 6 of 12  
**Statutory Topics Covered:** Railroad and Highway Grade Crossing Safety, Oversize and Overweight Vehicles, Litter Prevention

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this module, students will be able to:
- Adjust driving behavior for different weather and visibility conditions common in Texas
- Safely navigate construction zones and understand increased penalties
- Recognize and respond correctly to railroad and highway grade crossing signals
- Safely interact with oversize and overweight vehicles
- Understand litter prevention laws and environmental responsibility while driving

## Lesson Breakdown

### Lesson 6.1: Weather & Visibility (30 min)
- Rain, fog, ice, wind, and extreme Texas heat
- Hydroplaning prevention and recovery
- Night driving and glare management
- **Interactive:** "Weather Decision Simulator"

### Lesson 6.2: Construction Zones & Work Areas (35 min)
- Signs, flaggers, lane shifts, and speed reductions
- **Statutory Requirement:** Increased fines in work zones
- "Move Over or Slow Down" law
- Worker safety awareness
- **Interactive:** Construction zone scenario branching

### Lesson 6.3: Railroad & Highway Grade Crossings (30 min)
- **Statutory Requirement:** Railroad and Highway Grade Crossing Safety
- Signs, signals, and pavement markings at crossings
- What to do when signals activate
- Dangers of stopping on tracks
- **Interactive:** Crossing safety quiz + simulation

### Lesson 6.4: Oversize, Overweight & Large Vehicles (25 min)
- **Statutory Requirement:** Information Relating to Oversize and Overweight Vehicles
- Safe following distances and blind spots
- Passing procedures for trucks and farm equipment
- "Move Over" law application
- **Interactive:** "Share the Road" decision scenarios

### Lesson 6.5: Litter Prevention & Environmental Responsibility (20 min)
- **Statutory Requirement:** Information Relating to Litter Prevention
- Texas litter laws and fines
- "Don't Mess With Texas" campaign context
- Proper disposal while driving
- Environmental impact of vehicle-related litter

### Lesson 6.6: Module Review & Assessment (40 min)
- Comprehensive review
- Final Module 6 Assessment (18 questions)
- Emphasis on statutory topics

## Key Teaching Points (Texas-Specific)
- Construction zone fines are often doubled
- Texas has strict "Move Over" laws for emergency and maintenance vehicles
- Many fatal crashes at railroad crossings involve drivers ignoring signals
- Large trucks have significant blind spots — never drive in them
