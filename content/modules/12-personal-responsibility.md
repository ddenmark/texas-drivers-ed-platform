# Module 12: Personal Responsibility and Driving
**Target Duration:** 2.5 hours (150 minutes)  
**Position:** Final module  
**Statutory Topics Covered:** Motorcycle Awareness Information, Human Trafficking Prevention Information

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this module, students will be able to:
- Practice courtesy and manage road rage
- Safely share the road with motorcycles and other vulnerable users
- Recognize signs of human trafficking and know how to report it
- Understand the importance of lifelong safe driving habits

## Lesson Breakdown

### Lesson 12.1: Courtesy & Road Rage (25 min)
- Aggressive driving vs. defensive driving
- Managing emotions behind the wheel
- Courtesy behaviors that reduce conflict

### Lesson 12.2: Sharing the Road (35 min)
- Motorcycles, bicycles, and pedestrians
- **Statutory Requirement:** Motorcycle Awareness Information
- Large trucks and buses
- School buses and emergency vehicles
- **Interactive:** "Share the Road" hazard identification

### Lesson 12.3: Human Trafficking Prevention (30 min)
- **Statutory Requirement:** Human Trafficking Prevention Information
- What human trafficking looks like (signs at rest stops, hotels, etc.)
- How drivers might encounter it
- Safe reporting methods (hotlines, apps)
- Personal safety while reporting
- **Interactive:** Scenario-based recognition exercise

### Lesson 12.4: Lifelong Safe Driving & Commitment (25 min)
- Continuing education and refresher courses
- Personal safe driving commitment statement
- Insurance discounts for safe drivers
- Setting a good example for others

### Lesson 12.5: Course Wrap-Up & Final Assessment (35 min)
- Comprehensive final exam covering all 12 modules
- Course feedback
- Certificate issuance process

## Key Teaching Points
- Motorcyclists are much more vulnerable in crashes
- Human trafficking is more common than many people realize — awareness saves lives
- Safe driving is a lifelong skill that requires continuous attention

## Sample Assessment Items
1. What should you do if you suspect human trafficking?
2. Why are motorcyclists harder to see?
3. What are three ways to reduce road rage?

## Statutory Integration
Both Motorcycle Awareness and Human Trafficking Prevention are treated as important mandatory topics in the final module.
