# Module 2: Driver Preparation
**Target Duration:** 2.5 hours (150 minutes)  
**Position:** Module 2 of 12  
**Statutory Topics Covered:** None (foundational skills module)

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this module, students will be able to:
- Identify and properly use all vehicle controls and instruments
- Perform a thorough pre-trip inspection
- Adjust seating position, mirrors, and steering wheel for optimal control and visibility
- Demonstrate proper starting, stopping, and steering techniques

## Lesson Breakdown

### Lesson 2.1: Vehicle Controls & Instruments (30 min)
- Dashboard layout and warning lights
- Steering wheel controls, gear selector, pedals
- Turn signals, wipers, lights, and horn
- **Interactive:** Dashboard identification activity

### Lesson 2.2: Pre-Trip Inspection (35 min)
- Exterior walk-around (tires, lights, fluids, leaks)
- Interior checks (seat belts, mirrors, gauges)
- Under the hood basics (when safe)
- **Interactive:** Pre-trip inspection checklist simulator

### Lesson 2.3: Seating Position & Mirror Adjustment (25 min)
- Proper seating posture and distance from pedals/wheel
- Inside and outside mirror adjustment (blind spot minimization)
- Head restraint positioning
- **Interactive:** "Set Up Your Cockpit" guided exercise

### Lesson 2.4: Starting, Stopping & Steering (30 min)
- Starting the vehicle safely
- Smooth acceleration and braking
- Proper hand positioning and steering techniques (push-pull or hand-over-hand)
- Backing and turning basics

### Lesson 2.5: Module Review & Assessment (30 min)
- Final Module 2 Assessment
- Emphasis on building good habits from the start

## Key Teaching Points
- Proper seating and mirror setup dramatically reduces blind spots
- Many new drivers skip pre-trip inspections — this is a critical safety habit
- Smooth inputs (steering, braking, acceleration) improve control and fuel efficiency

## Sample Assessment Items
1. What is the correct hand position on the steering wheel?
2. Name three items you should check during a pre-trip inspection.
3. Why is proper mirror adjustment important?
